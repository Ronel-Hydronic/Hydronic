About HYDRONIC X1000

HYDRONIC X1000 is an advanced agriculture rover designed to monitor soil health by calculating the pH value and dispensing the right amount of fertilizer. It aims to revolutionize the way we approach farming, ensuring optimal crop growth and sustainable agricultural practices.

Features

  • Automated Soil pH Measurement
  • Intelligent Fertilizer Dispensing
  • Real-time Soil Data Monitoring
  • Easy Integration with Existing Farm Systems
  • Environmentally Friendly and Sustainable Design
